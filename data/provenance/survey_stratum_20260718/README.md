# Survey composition + classified-FRB stratification products (2026-07-18)

Generated by analysis/survey_composition.py from inventory.jsonl +
event_presence_keys.csv.gz + the per-frame dump (see the script
docstring for the unit->event assignment method and its measured
validation). survey_assignment_quality.csv reports per-channel
assignment completeness (denominators for the provisional-rates
caveat); survey_quarterly_exposure.csv carries per-quarter unit and
FRB-unit denominators.

survey_quarterly_rates_all23.csv (added same day) is the completeness
scan: quarterly hi-rates for EVERY calibrated channel (all events +
classified.FRB stratum, 40-valid-frame floor; refused ch24/ch30
excluded -- no calibrated zero point to rate against). It backs the
Sec. 6.3 statement that exactly one further multi-year transition
exists below the episodic tail criterion (ch34: 3--8 per cent
2018--2020, ~2 per cent through 2021, <~1 per cent after, in both
strata) plus one sampled loud interval on a capped channel (ch27:
38 per cent in 2020 Q3, 37 per cent in the FRB stratum, quiet at the
2025--26 endpoint, interior unsampled), and the Sec. 8.1 per-tier
flatness statements (ch20/ch22 flat-quiet; the remaining
first-adoption six at the 1--2 per cent level with no sustained
excursion in any sampled quarter).

event_presence_keys.csv.gz (added 2026-07-20): the product-presence
bitmask dump the survey scripts join against; committed so the survey
chain reproduces from a bare clone (PP_EVENTKEYS defaults to this
copy).
